Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

  • Your uploaded PDFs and parsed course data are never stored on our servers after processing. They are used only to generate your calendar events and are deleted immediately after your session ends.
  • If you choose to enter your email, it is used only to send you a summary of your calendar events. We may store your email address to notify you of important updates, but we will never share or sell your email.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or analytics purposes.
  • You can contact us at chris@chrislawrence.com for any privacy concerns or questions.
